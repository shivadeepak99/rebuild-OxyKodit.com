Spancat: A New Approach for Span Labeling

Introducing SpanCategorizer - a revolutionary spaCy component for structured annotation of overlapping spans, long phrases, and complex entity relationships

Span Labeling Overlapping Annotations spaCy Component NLP Innovation Structured Annotation

Revolutionizing Span Annotation

The SpanCategorizer is a new spaCy component that answers the NLP community's need to have structured annotation for a wide variety of labeled spans, including long phrases, non-named entities, or overlapping annotations.

In this blog post, we talk more about spancat and showcase new features to help with your span labeling needs!

Overlapping Spans in Action

bacteremia originating from lower respiratory tract infection

Condition: "bacteremia"
Factor: "bacteremia originating from lower respiratory tract infection"

Example: The word "bacteremia" is labeled both as a "Condition" and as part of a larger "Factor" phrase. Traditional NER systems struggle with such overlapping annotations.
