<h1 style="text-align: left">1. AI proof-of-concept</h1>
<p class="body">
<ul style="list-style: square; text-align: left; color: #324a51; margin-left: 1em">
<li>Perform a thorough analysis of a specific business case, including performance requirements.</li>
<li>Analyse the available data sources in terms of quantity, quality and predictive signal.</li>
<li>Implement an annotation framework to produce, in collaboration with the business partners, a realistic training and/or evaluation dataset.</li>
<li>Determine a suitable strategy to coordinate relevant LLM models, NLP algorithms and data integration methods.</li>
<li>Analyse the trade-off between using closed LLMs (through an external API) or open-source models (deployed in-house).</li>
<li><u>Example data sources</u>: news, <a href="blog/evex"><u>research articles</u></a>, patents, contracts, <a href="blog/az_2024"><u>clinical trials</u></a>, physician notes and EMRs, social media, customer requests, …</li>
<li><u>Example NLP components</u>:</li>
<ul style="list-style: circle; text-align: left; color: #324a51; margin-left: 1em">
<li>Text summarization and question answering</li>
<li>Multi-linguality and Machine Translation</li>
<li><a href="blog/spancat/"><u>Named entity recognition</u></a> (NER) and <a href="blog/el_tutorial/"><u>normalization</u></a> (EL) to disambiguate textual mentions to unique identifiers</li>
<li><a href="blog/rel_tutorial/"><u>Relation extraction</u></a> and <a href="blog/bionlp09/">event extraction</a></li>
<li><a href="blog/coref_experimental/"><u>Coreference resolution</u></a>: grouping references to the same person or object</li>
<li>Document classification (textcat) &amp; sentiment analysis</li>
</ul>
<li><u>Example projects I have worked on</u>:</li>
<ul style="list-style: circle; text-align: left; color: #324a51; margin-left: 1em">
<li>Implemented a large-scale text mining framework called <a href="blog/evex/"><u>EVEX</u></a> to identify biomolecular events in millions of research articles.</li>
<li>Analysed a set of legal documents to identify similar paragraphs and sentences, and used NLP and clustering techniques to implement a template generator that can significantly reduce required editing time for a new document.</li>
<li>Designed and implemented an NLP strategy to mine diagnostic reports, identify relevant information and summarize patient characteristics through named entity recognition and relation extraction techniques.</li>
<li>Implemented the data model and helped design the graphical user interface for an <a href="blog/knowntator/"><u>annotation framework</u></a> focused on capturing the molecular mechanisms of leaf growth and development in the Arabidopsis plant.</li>
<li>Created a novel framework <a href="blog/diffany/"><u>Diffany</u></a> to analyse the rewiring of biomolecular interactions under stress conditions such as plant drought or human cancer.</li>
<li>Implemented an optimization framework to create hyper-personalized <a href="blog/cocktail_generator/"><u>cocktails and mocktails</u></a> according to user preferences.</li>
</ul>

<li>Identify structural errors (if any) in the dataset and/or annotation guidelines.</li>

<li><a href="blog/ml_success/"><u>Iterate</u></a> on both data and the ML models.</li>

<ul style="list-style: circle; text-align: left; color: #324a51; margin-left: 1em">

<li>Experimented with various hyperparameter tuning experiments as well as different architectures to optimize F-score of supervised ML models.</li>
<li>Performed a critical evaluation of existing tools to recognize mentions of <a href="blog/cell_lines/"><u>cell lines</u></a> in text, and developed two new annotated datasets to further boost development of NLP algorithms in this domain.</li>
